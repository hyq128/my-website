My hobbies

Basketball

I like to watch basketball games, and my favorite team is Guangdong Hongyuan. The excitement of each match, the skillful plays, and the incredible teamwork displayed by the players make watching basketball an exhilarating experience. Guangdong Hongyuan, with its rich history and numerous championships, has always captivated my attention. The team's dedication, strategic prowess, and never-give-up attitude resonate with me deeply. I admire the way they synchronize their efforts on the court, turning individual talents into a cohesive unit that consistently delivers outstanding performances.

Badminton

Badminton is one of my favorite sports, and I enjoy the fun of every game. The thrill of each rally, the strategic placement of shots, and the exhilaration of a well-executed smash all contribute to my love for the game. Playing badminton not only keeps me physically fit but also sharpens my reflexes and enhances my mental agility.

Piano

Playing the piano makes me feel relaxed and happy, and it is an indispensable part of my life. When my fingers glide over the keys, the stress of the day melts away, and I find myself immersed in the beautiful melodies. Each note resonates with my emotions, allowing me to express myself in ways words cannot.

Food

I like to try various delicacies, especially hot pot. Hot pot not only offers a rich selection of ingredients, but also unique seasoning combinations, which can bring different surprises to the taste buds every time. Hot pot is a very social way of dining, where you sit around with family and friends, eat and chat while enjoying yourself.
